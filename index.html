CLUB WORKING PROCEDURE

  • Club conducts regular classes after college hours, students are trained on their chosen domains.
  • Coding Session works in parallel on weekends, to improve problem solving skills.
  • Individual and Team Projects are assigned each semester.
  • Hackathon Participation opens the gate for analysing and debugging real-life challenges.
  • Inter-domains Projects are developed by blending different technologies together.
  • Placement Modules are organized for prefinal year students.
